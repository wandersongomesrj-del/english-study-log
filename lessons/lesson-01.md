# Lesson 01: Pronunciation Hacks 🗣️

In this lesson, I learned about **Common Contractions** to sound more natural and fluent.

### 1. Common Contractions (Verb to Be)
* **I am** → I'm  /aɪm/
* **You are** → You're /jʊər/
* **That is** → That's /ðæts/

### 2. Fast Questions
* **What's wrong?** (Instead of "What is wrong?")
* **What're you doing?** (Instead of "What are you doing?")

### 3. Informal/Regional Variations (Bonus)
* **Y'all're** (You all are - very common in the Southern US)
* **Guys're** (The guys are)
